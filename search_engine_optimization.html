    Keyword Analysis
    It may at first seem like a waste of time to go through your content and analyze each and every keyword being used but this is the key to Search Engine Optimization. Google and Overture offer keyword search tools that show you which keywords are most highly searched for within your specific niche and these can then be inserted into your content. Google and other top search engines rely on keyword analysis to decide which sites to bring up with every search made.
